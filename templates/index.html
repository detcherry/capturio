WHAT IS CAPTUR.IO?

Captur.io is an application where you send an image of a person’s object (his captur.io object) by email and receive instantly the vcard that he had linked with it.

With Captur.io, you can:
- Associate any personal and original object (business card, student ID or even your t-shirt, tattoo...etc) to your vcard
- Receive by email people’s vcard by taking a picture of their personal Captur.io object.

HOW DOES IT WORK?

How to associate my personal object to my vcard?

- Take a picture of one of your personal and original object (it can be ANYTHING)
- Send your pic by email to post@captur.io
- Send also your vcard to post@captur.io. (You can do both separately or at the same time)
You're all set! You can start letting people taking a pic of your captur.io object to receive your vcard.

How to retrieve people's vcard?

- Take a picture of someone's captur.io object.
- Send it to ping@captur.io
- You'll receive instantly his vcard!
