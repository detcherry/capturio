HOW TO GET SET UP?

With Captur.io, you can:
- Associate one of your personal and original object to your vcard
- Receive by email people’s vcard.
Look below to know how to master Capturio

HOW TO ASSOCIATE MY OBJECT TO MY VCARD?

1) TAKE A PIC OF YOUR OBJECT

Pick whatever you want! A business card, a t-shirt, a sticker, a badge, a card or a tattoo. Classic or creative, it's up to you!

2) SEND IT TO POST@CAPTUR.IO

Ok, go to step 3) now!

3) SEND YOUR VCARD TO POST@CAPTUR.IO

Ok, you’re fully set up! You can now let people take a pic of your object to receive your vcard
PS: if you’re smart you can also do step 2 and 3 at the same time ;-)

HOW TO RETRIEVE SOMEONE'S VCARD?

1) TAKE A PIC OF HIS OBJECT

This person should already have sent his vcard and capturio object to post@captur.io

2) SEND IT TO GET@CAPTUR.IO

That's all you need to do.

3) YOU'LL RECEIVE INSTANTLY HIS VCARD

Pretty cool, isn't it?
