HOW TO GET SET UP?

With Captur.io, you can:
- Associate your favorite t-shirt to your contact information
- Receive by email people’s contac information.
Look below to know how to master Capturio

HOW TO ASSOCIATE MY T-SHIRT TO MY CONTACT INFO?

1) TAKE A PIC OF YOUR T-SHIRT

Pick your most beautiful tshirt! The one you love so much that your friends keep asking where you bought it!

2) SEND IT TO POST@CAPTUR.IO

Ok, go to step 3) now!

3) SEND YOUR INFO TO POST@CAPTUR.IO

Ok, you’re fully set up! You can now let people take a pic of your tshirt to receive your contact info
PS: if you’re smart you can also do step 2 and 3 at the same time ;-)

HOW TO RETRIEVE SOMEONE'S CONTACT INFO?

1) TAKE A PIC OF HIS T-SHIRT

This person should already have sent his tshirt and contact information to post@captur.io

2) SEND IT TO GET@CAPTUR.IO

That's all you need to do.

3) YOU GET INSTANTLY HIS CONTACT INFO

Pretty cool, isn't it?
