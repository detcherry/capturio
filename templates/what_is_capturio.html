WHAT IS CAPTURIO?

Capturio is an application where you send an image of a person’s t-shirt by email and receive instantly the contact information that he had linked with it.
For more details see how to get set up with Capturio

BASICALLY

OUR TECH RECOGNIZES IMAGES

That’s how we do this magic trick.You send an image and you receive some contact information (phone number, email address...). In the middle there are highly accurate, highly robust, highly fast algorithms that had been developed for years.

IT’S CROSS PLATFORM

You just need a camera and email in your phone! So it works on iPhone, Android, Blackberry, and more!

IT’S DEAD SIMPLE LIKE EMAIL

- Send your contact information and a picture of your favorite t-shirt to post@captur.io to set up your profile.
- Send a pic of someone’s t-shirt to get@captur.io and receive instantly his contact information by email.

IT’S FREE, THERE IS NO SIGN UP
