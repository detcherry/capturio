WHAT IS CAPTURIO?

Capturio is an application where you send an image of a person’s object by email and receive instantly the vcard that he had linked with it.
For more details see how to get set up with Capturio

BASICALLY

OUR TECH RECOGNIZES IMAGES

That’s how we do this magic trick.You send an image and you receive a vcard. In the middle there are highly accurate, highly robust, highly fast algorithms that had been developed for years.

YOU CAN LINK WHATEVER YOU WANT

Capturio possibilities are limitless. You can link your vcard to your physical business card or be a little more creative. You can link your t-shirt, your logos, your visuals, a picture even a tattoo!

IT’S CROSS PLATFORM

You just need a camera and email in your phone! So it works on iPhone, Android, Blackberry, and more!

IT’S DEAD SIMPLE LIKE EMAIL

- Send your vcard and a pic of your personal and original object to post@captur.io to set up your profile.
- Send a pic of someone’s capturio object to get@captur.io and receive instantly his vcard by email.

IT’S FREE, THERE IS NO SIGN UP
