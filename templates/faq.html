FAQ

If you have any question, there might be your answer in this section.
Didn't find yours? please contact us here. We'll get back to you as soon as possible.

HOW TO LINK MY VCARD TO ANY OBJECT?

Send your vcard and an image of your object to post@captur.io. If you need more explanations, go to the how to get set up section. There is especially a video tutorial.

HOW TO GET SOMEONE'S VCARD?

Just take a picture of his capturio object (the one that he had linked to his vcard on capturio). And send it to get@captur.io. You'll receive his vcard almost instantly. If you need more explanations, go to the how it works section.

HOW TO EDIT MY INFORMATION/ CHANGE MY CAPTURIO OBJECT OR VCARD?

Just send a new mail to post@captur.io with your new information (your new vcard and/or image of your new object). You will receive a confirmation link. If you click on it, your old information will be removed and the new information will be added to your Capturio profile.

HOW TO PERMANENTLY DELETE MY INFORMATION FROM CAPTURIO?

Just send an email to delete@captur.io. You will receive a confirmation link and, if you click on it, your information will be removed from Capturio (your vcard and/or image). Of course, you can sign up again anytime you want.
