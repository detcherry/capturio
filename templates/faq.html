FAQ

If you have any question, there might be your answer in this section.
Didn't find yours? please contact us here. We'll get back to you as soon as possible.

HOW TO LINK MY INFO TO MY T-SHIRT?

Send your contact info and an image of your tshirt to post@captur.io. If you need more explanations, go to the how to get set up section.

HOW TO GET SOMEONE'S CONTACT INFO?

Just take a picture of his tshirt (the one that he had linked to his contact info on capturio). And send it to get@captur.io. You'll receive instantly his contact info. If you need more explanations, go to the how it works section.

HOW TO EDIT MY INFORMATION/ CHANGE MY TSHIRT OR CONTACT INFO?

Just send a new mail to post@captur.io with your new information (your new contact information and/or image of your new tshirt). You will receive a confirmation link. If you click on it, your old information will be removed and the new information will be added to your Capturio profile.

HOW TO PERMANENTLY DELETE MY INFORMATION FROM CAPTURIO?

Just send an email to delete@captur.io. You will receive a confirmation link and, if you click on it, your information will be removed from Capturio (your contact info and/or image). Of course, you can sign up again anytime you want.

HOW MANY TSHIRTS CAN I LINK TO MY CONTACT INFO?

At the moment it's just one. But in a very near future, we will increase the number of tshirts you can add per user.

Of course, we know you change your tshirt everyday! We don't want you to adopt dirty habits because of Capturio.

What you can do: Each time you change your tshirt, just send a picture of your new tshirt to post@captur.io. The old one will be replaced and you'll have another brand new business card that other people can start snapping.

HOW TO KNOW WHICH TSHIRT I'VE LINKED TO MY CONTACT INFO?

Just send an email to status@captur.io and we will send you the image that you've linked with your contact info.
